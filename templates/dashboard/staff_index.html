Our Location

Santa Magdalena is a municipality located in the province of Sorsogon in the Philippines. It is a picturesque coastal town situated on the southeastern part of Luzon island. The town is known for its natural beauty, with pristine beaches, lush forests, and a serene ambiance. Santa Magdalena offers a tranquil escape for those seeking a peaceful retreat in a tropical paradise. Its economy is primarily based on agriculture, fishing, and tourism, making it an ideal destination for travelers looking to explore the unspoiled beauty of the Philippines.
